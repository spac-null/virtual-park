Four attempts at
the same place

A park built from code. Each version is a different way of being there. The park is the same in all four. Only the way in changes.

01 The Walking Version Low-poly, real-time

A low-polygon world where a bird collects what the park left behind. Gems appear where benches might have been.

Arrow keys or WASD to move — Space to jump
→
02 The Hovering Version Bird's eye, tap to fly

The whole park seen from the height where sound stops. Tap anywhere to fly there.

Touch or click to move — the park turns beneath you
→
03 The Remembering Version Memory traces, persistent

Leave a note at a coordinate. Read what others pinned to thin air. The park as a container for things almost said.

Click a spot — type — it stays
→
04 The Storied Version Currently sealed

The same ground, but someone lived here. Paths have names. Corners have reasons.
